ABOUT US

We’re Julia & Gil, a married couple living in a charming old apartment together with our beloved daughter. We’re in business for nearly a decade and have had the privilege of capturing weddings in countries including Italy, France, Portugal, Switzerland, Spain, the Netherlands, Germany and Austria.

Our work has been featured in prestigious publications such as Vogue, The Wed, Junebug Weddings, Anti-Bride, Magnolia Rouge, Style Me Pretty, The Lane and Hochzeitswahn.

We’re proud ambassadors for leading brands in the photography industry, including Canon, PicTime, Pixellu, Narrative and Imagen.
